Placement Training Program

Duration: 6 Weeks

Weeks 1-2: Soft Skills and Communication

  • Week 1: Effective Communication Skills
    • Importance of communication in the workplace
    • Verbal and non-verbal communication
    • Business communication etiquette
  • Week 2: Presentation and Public Speaking
    • Structuring effective presentations
    • Techniques for public speaking confidence
    • Handling Q&A sessions professionally

Weeks 3-4: Interview Preparation and Professional Development

  • Week 3: Resume Building and LinkedIn Profile Optimization
    • Crafting a compelling resume
    • Creating an impactful LinkedIn profile
    • Showcasing achievements and skills
  • Week 4: Behavioral and Technical Interview Techniques
    • Understanding behavioral interview questions
    • Handling common technical interview scenarios
    • Problem-solving and critical thinking exercises

Weeks 5-6: Advanced Technical Skills and Mock Interviews

  • Week 5: Advanced Technical Skills Enhancement
    • In-depth review of programming languages and frameworks
    • System design concepts
    • Code optimization and best practices
  • Week 6: Mock Interviews and Feedback Sessions
    • Conducting multiple rounds of mock interviews
    • Providing detailed feedback and improvement suggestions
    • Addressing common interview challenges

Additional Aspects:

  • Group Discussions and Case Studies
  • Industry Insights and Guest Speakers
  • Networking and Job Search Strategies
  • Continuous Learning Resources
  • Placement Assistance
  • Certification of Completion

Note:

This extended plan provides more time for in-depth exploration of technical concepts and additional practical exercises. Regularly solicit feedback from participants to tailor the training to their needs. Foster a supportive learning environment, encouraging collaboration and peer learning.
